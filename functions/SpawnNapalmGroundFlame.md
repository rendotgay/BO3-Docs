# SpawnNapalmGroundFlame

Spawn a napalm ground flame at the origin with the direction.

Example:
```c++
self SpawnNapalmGroundFlame( bot_pos , weapon, forward)
```

### Parameters
Parameter | Summary
| - | - |
*origin | The origin of the flame
*weapon | The weapon
*direction | The direction of the flame
[time] | The time to live

<sub>* = mandatory</sub>
