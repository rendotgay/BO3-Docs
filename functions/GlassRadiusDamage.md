# GlassRadiusDamage

Damages glass in the radius

Example:
```c++
GlassRadiusDamage( origin, range, max_damage, min_damage, means_of_damage )
```

### Parameters
Parameter | Summary
| - | - |
*origin | Origin of damage
*radius | Radius of damage
*max_damage | Max damage, this is the damage at the origin
*min_damage | Max damage, this is the damage at the edge of the radius
[means_of_death] | Means of death passed to the glass system from script, defaults to MOD_EXPLOSIVE

<sub>* = mandatory</sub>
